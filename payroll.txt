Attendance is marked with the when the users face is recognized
Absceteeism attract a deduction of GHC 1.20 from the basic salary
The payroll will be calculated as:

Basic salary

Allowances
1. Overtime
2. Rent 
3. Spouse

Total allowances = 1 + 2+3

Deductions
4.Absenteeism
5. SSNIT 
6. Welfare

SSNIT = (14.5 / 100) x basic salary

Total deduction = 4 +5+6

Gross salary = Basic salary + Total Allowances + Total deduction 

Net salary = Basic salary + Total allowances
Payroll will then be printed
